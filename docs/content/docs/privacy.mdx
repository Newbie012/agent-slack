agent-slack is a local CLI. Slack tokens are stored on your machine and are
never sent to this site.

## OAuth relay

When you sign in, this site receives Slack's OAuth callback parameters and
immediately forwards them to the CLI's callback on your machine. It is a
pass-through step, not a place your data is kept.

## Stored data

The relay stores no Slack messages, files, tokens, workspace data, or user data.

## Slack access

The CLI can read only the Slack data your approved scopes, workspace policy, and
channel membership allow.

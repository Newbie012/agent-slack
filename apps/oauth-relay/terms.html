agent-slack is a free, open-source command-line tool for reading Slack from your terminal. By installing the Slack app or using the CLI and its hosted OAuth relay, you agree to these terms.

The service

The service consists of the agent-slack CLI, which runs entirely on your machine, and a stateless OAuth relay that forwards Slack's login callback to your localhost. There is no other backend. The service is provided free of charge.

The hosted relay is provided on a best-effort basis and may change or be discontinued at any time. Login does not depend on it: the CLI supports a localhost redirect that bypasses the relay entirely. These terms may be updated; the current version is always at this URL.
